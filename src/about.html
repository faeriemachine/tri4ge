üè• Why Triage?

I came up with the name Triage due to wanting to associate my art alias and branding with medical sciences and imagery. As someone who is both disabled and a medical trauma survivor, I like to think of this as being my attempt at reclaiming medical themes as a source of empowerment and comfort. Admittedly, it's a bit hard to explain how I find comfort in things like medical science and hospitals to people who haven't experienced this kind of trauma, but I find those who have just get it immediately.
